About RetainIQ

A premium AI platform that transforms churn risk into actionable retention strategies using MLOps and real-time insights.

What is Customer Churn?

Customer churn, also known as customer attrition, occurs when customers stop doing business with a company. It is a critical metric for businesses because acquiring new customers costs significantly more than retaining existing ones.

Understanding churn helps businesses identify at-risk customers early and implement retention strategies to keep them engaged and satisfied.

What is Machine Learning?

Machine Learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. In this project, we use ML algorithms to predict customer churn based on historical data patterns.

Our model analyzes various customer features to identify patterns that indicate a likelihood of churn, enabling proactive retention actions.

What is MLOps?

MLOps (Machine Learning Operations) is a set of practices that combines machine learning, DevOps, and data engineering to deploy and maintain ML models in production reliably and efficiently.

This project implements MLOps principles including:

  • Continuous model training and evaluation
  • Model versioning and tracking
  • Automated deployment pipeline
  • Real-time monitoring and logging
  • Scalable infrastructure

Dataset Information

Our model is trained on a comprehensive customer dataset containing:

  • Customer demographics (gender, age, dependents)
  • Account information (tenure, contract type, payment method)
  • Services subscribed (internet, phone, streaming)
  • Billing details (monthly charges, total charges)
  • Churn status (target variable)

Dataset size: 7,043 customers with 21 features.

Algorithms Used

We tested multiple algorithms and selected the best performing ones:

Random Forest XGBoost Logistic Regression SVM Gradient Boosting

The final model achieved 95% accuracy on the test set.
